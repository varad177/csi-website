ABOUT CSI

Welcome to CSI DMCE

CSI DMCE is a student branch of Computer Science and Information Engineering (CSCE) affiliated to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (DMCE), Pune, India. Established in the year 2012, CSI DMCE aims to foster experiences, teamwork, networking, and collaboration among its students.
