ABOUT CSI-CATT DMCE

Established in 2012, we are the Computer Society of India's Student Branch at DMCE, dedicated to fostering technical excellence and innovation.

Our Vision

To provide an intellectually stimulating environment for education, technological excellence in Computer Engineering field, and professional training along with human values.

" सर्वे भवन्तु सुखंन्ति "
"We strive to create an environment where students can collaborate, innovate, and lead within a vibrant technological community, preparing for impactful careers."

Prof. Vaishali.k.Gaidhane

Faculty Co-ordinator

Our Mission

To promote an educational environment that combines academics with intellectual curiosity.
To develop human resources with sound knowledge of theory and practical in the discipline of Computer Engineering and the ability to apply the knowledge to the benefit of society at large.
To assimilate creative research and new technologies in order to facilitate students to be lifelong learners who will contribute positively to the economic well-being of the nation.

Welcome to the CSI DMCE Student Chapter, dedicated to fostering innovation and excellence in the Computer Engineering Department. Established in 2012-13, CSI DMCE is equipped with cutting-edge infrastructure to support a world-class learning environment. Our faculty, averaging 12 years of expertise, are encouraged to pursue higher education, research, and adopt innovative teaching methodologies.We prioritize active interaction among students, parents, and staff, ensuring a supportive and collaborative environment. CSI COORDINATIOR

CSI DMCE - Student Branch, established in 2012, is a student-driven committee that plays a vital role in organizing workshops, seminars, technical competitions, and the annual Technical Fest in collaboration with the Computer Association for Technological Trends (CATT). The committee focuses on creating opportunities for students to enhance their skills, work in teams, and build strong networks within the college. By providing a platform for peer learning and collaboration, CSI DMCE encourages students to engage in hands-on projects, explore new technologies, and develop leadership qualities. The initiatives promote a spirit of innovation and problem-solving, helping students grow both technically and personally.
